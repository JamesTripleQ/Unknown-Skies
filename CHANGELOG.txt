W.I.P. Version 2.0.0 - [date]
- Minor text fixes.
- Externalized table strings.
- Tables now show which stat is scaling.
- Unique planetary conditions (Artificial, Magnetic, Windswept) are now always the rightmost.
- Buffed Military Virus:
    . Weapon production is now +4 (was +3).
    . Ground defense is now -40% (was -50%).
    . Population growth is now (colony size + 1) * -2 (was colony size * -3).
- Unique conditions (Shrooms, Spores, Virus) will now prevent other US special conditions from spawning (planetary conditions and crystals are unaffected).
- Fixed a rare bug that caused Decivilized to spawn without ruins on gas giants with Floating Continent.
- Added Hycean worlds (special thanks to TouchOfVanilla for the original idea and lots of feedback).
- Methane worlds now always have organics.
- Added a new unique planet: Fluorescent Giant.
- Added LunaLib Version Checker params.
- Added new easter egg name.
- Completely restructured the spawn system for certain planets:
    . Fixed US_volcanic having the stats of lava instead of lava_minor
    . Fixed cat_hab3 being a mess (Terran Eccentric worlds now have actually fair spawn chances again)
    . Swapped categories for US_lifeless and US_crimson
    . Renamed Arid Lifeless to Lifeless (since it's the same as US_lifeless now)
    . Fixed US_crimson not matching the spawn weights of US_lifeless
- Added Savannah and Alpine worlds (TexturesForPlanets).
- Added solar clouds
- Fixed icon color of Archipelago and Methane worlds.

Version 1.0.11 - 26/04/2024
- Added an expandable tooltip to conditions with scaling that shows the strength of the effect at the various market sizes.
- Conditions that scale with market size now have a scaling cap:
    . Markets of size 2 and lower will always have the size 3 effect.
    . Markets of size 7 and higher will always have the size 6 effect.
- Added the vanilla condition images used by the mod to the files in case they get removed in the future.
- Unique planets and conditions can no longer spawn on the Planet Killer and Planetary Shield planets.
- Unique planets now have the proper TypeId.
- Minor text rewording.
- Added a new easter egg name.
